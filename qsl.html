Direct QSLs

Direct QSLs should use the ClubLog OQRS (with PayPal donation to cover postage costs), or be sent to: Robert Chipperfield, 13 Harlestones Road, Cottenham, Cambridge, CB24 8TR, England

Bureau requests

Please use the ClubLog OQRS to request your card. There is no charge for bureau cards. Alternatively, please email M0VFC (address listed on QRZ.com). Please do not send cards via the bureau system - we don't need your card, and it only puts undue load on the system. There is no ZD9 QSL bureau!.
